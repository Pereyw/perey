Biography

MY PASSION FOR BSc. INFORMATION TECHNOLGY Perey Kuu-Eru is my name from the family of eight of which I am the lastborn of family. From Lawra Yikpee in the upper West region of Ghana, schooled at Akumadan D/A “A” JHS completed in 2016, proceeded to Queen of Peace SHS where I offered General Science and completed successfully in 2019 and now perusing a program in BSc. Information Technology in CKT-UTAS
Upon all these experiences I had from people and other personal research that I also searched about the field of IT has given me more inspiration to learn more of IT in the future. Now cyber security is also trending in the field and after finishing my degree program, which is my next line of study I will like to dive deep into because the systems created today needs security measures as well as the internet also needs security measures. It is not because of the money in the system that I love but the fact that I can protect and defend people from danger using the knowledge that I will gain from my field as an IT specialist.
Day in day out technology keeps on advancing and updating, being updated always to new content of the world makes IT personnel different from others as it makes them problem solvers and critical thinkers. I love the updates and I always want to advance my knowledge in the field of Information Technology.
In simple put it is through People who are into IT, technological advancement, personal exposure and feelings that motivated me in studying the program BSc. Information
